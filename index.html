Business Description – NextGen Consulting

NextGen Consulting is a professional consulting firm dedicated to providing end-to-end compliance, payroll, and financial advisory services to businesses across India. We specialize in statutory compliance solutions such as EPF, ESIC, Maharashtra Professional Tax (MPT), and a wide range of finance and labor law consulting services.

Our mission is to simplify complex regulatory requirements and help organizations operate smoothly, compliantly, and efficiently. We act as a trusted partner for startups, SMEs, contractors, and large organizations by managing statutory obligations with accuracy, transparency, and timeliness.

Our Vision

To become a trusted compliance and finance consulting partner by delivering reliable, future-ready solutions that help businesses grow confidently in a regulated environment.

Our Mission

To simplify compliance, reduce administrative burden, and enable our clients to focus on their core business operations.
